Anti-D Immunoglobulin

Essential Information for Rh-Negative Mothers

What is Anti-D?

Anti-D immunoglobulin is a life-saving injection given to Rh-negative pregnant women to prevent Rh incompatibility complications.

Who Needs It?
  • Rh-negative mothers (blood group A-, B-, AB-, O-)
  • Carrying or delivered an Rh-positive baby
  • After miscarriage or abortion
